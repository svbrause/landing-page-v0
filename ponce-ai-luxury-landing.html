Quick answers

Quick answers

What does the patient actually have to do?

She clicks a link on her phone, takes a quick selfie, and gets a personalized facial assessment with treatment recommendations matched to her anatomy. Takes about 60 seconds.

What does my team have to do?

Almost nothing. Send the patient a link before her appointment. During the consult, glance at the dashboard to see what she's interested in.

Do I need any hardware or equipment?

No. Zero hardware. Ponce AI works on any smartphone. No cameras, devices, dedicated room, or IT support needed.

Will this feel pushy to my patients?

The opposite. Patients explore at their own pace and choose what interests them. Nobody pushes anything.

How long does setup take?

Days, not weeks. There is nothing to install or integrate upfront. Most practices are live within the first week.

What does it cost?

It's a monthly software subscription, a small fraction of what hardware systems cost. We'll show exact math on the demo call based on patient volume.

Can I try it before I commit?

Yes. Every new practice starts with a free pilot: 5 patients, 30 days, zero cost.
